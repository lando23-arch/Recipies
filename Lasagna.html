Lasagna

A classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. Prepare the meat sauce.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  4. Bake in oven for 40 minutes.
  5. Let cool and serve.
