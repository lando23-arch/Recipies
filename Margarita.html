Virgin Margarita

A refreshing non-alcoholic margarita with a citrus twist.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 1 cup ice
  • Salt for rim

Instructions

  1. Prepare glass with salt rim.
  2. Add ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Pour into glass.
  5. Serve cold.
