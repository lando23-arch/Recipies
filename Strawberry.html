Strawberry Lemonade

A sweet and refreshing drink perfect for hot days.

Ingredients

  • Fresh strawberries
  • Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Cold water

Instructions

  1. Blend strawberries.
  2. Add lemon juice and sugar.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Serve with ice.
