Mango Smoothie

A creamy tropical smoothie made with ripe mango.

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe mango
  • 1 cup milk
  • Ice cubes
  • Honey

Instructions

  1. Cut mango into pieces.
  2. Add all ingredients to blender.
  3. Blend until smooth.
  4. Serve chilled.
