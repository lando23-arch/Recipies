Sushi

A popular Japanese dish made with rice, seaweed, and fresh fillings.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Nori seaweed
  • Fresh fish or crab
  • Cucumber
  • Avocado
  • Soy sauce

Instructions

  1. Cook the sushi rice.
  2. Place nori on bamboo mat.
  3. Add rice and fillings.
  4. Roll tightly.
  5. Slice and serve.
